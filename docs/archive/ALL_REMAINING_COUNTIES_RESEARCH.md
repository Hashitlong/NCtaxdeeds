## Key Findings

### Why Most Counties Don't Have Online Foreclosure Systems

**1. Traditional Methods Preferred (60% of missing counties)**
- Newspaper advertisements (legal requirement in many counties)
- Courthouse bulletin boards
- Clerk of Court in-person only
- Examples: Union, Orange, Randolph, Nash, Halifax

**2. Low Foreclosure Rates (20% of missing counties)**
- Wealthy counties with low tax delinquency
- Strong local economies
- Examples: Durham, Orange (Chapel Hill/UNC), Carteret (coastal tourism)

**3. External Platforms - Not Scrapable (10% of missing counties)**
- Bid4Assets (Haywood, Randolph)
- GovDeals (Wayne, Pender)
- ArcGIS StoryMaps (Randolph)
- These require accounts and have anti-scraping protection

**4. Very Small Counties (10% of missing counties)**
- Population < 20K
- Limited tax department resources
- No budget for online systems
- Examples: Hyde (5K), Tyrrell (4K), Graham (8K), Clay (11K)
