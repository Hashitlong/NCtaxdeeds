# Missing NC Counties Report

**Date:** November 3, 2025  
**Coverage:** 79 out of 100 NC counties (79%)  
**Missing:** 21 counties

---

## Executive Summary

This report identifies the 21 North Carolina counties currently not covered by the NC Tax Deed Property Tracker and provides detailed explanations for why each cannot be scraped, along with monitoring strategies for when data becomes available.

### Key Findings

**Breakdown by Reason:**
- **Offline Publication Only:** 13 counties (62%)
- **Not Covered by Existing Scrapers:** 6 counties (29%)
- **No Active Foreclosures:** 2 counties (9%)

**Law Firm Coverage:**
- **Kania Law Firm:** 7 counties (but not all actively listed)
- **Ridenour & Goss, P.A.:** 3 counties
- **Other/Unknown:** 11 counties
